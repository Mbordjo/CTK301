About

Willy was the outcome of a school project I had to do in the intro to my major course. The project involved working with mediums we don't use often or don't like using and making something that embodied an emotion. So I went to the five below with a "friend", at the time, to get a canvas and watercolor paint. That is how Willy (or Willy the Whimsical Whale, as my peers called him) was born. I did this assignment at the last minute due to unseen events. So, less than an hour before class, I started making swirls, not knowing what to do. Then one swirl started looking like a fish, so I kept going with it and made eyes. I asked one of my online friends for a name, and they told me "Willy." Since then, Willy has been one of my favorite traditional art pieces because the event that happened before making him was very negative, and painting Willy was an escape from it.

I would love to do more artistic swirl pieces using paint in the future because that was fun and an excellent way to decompress.
