About

Shattered Reality was the first game idea I tried to put pen to paper and produce. It was supposed to be for my final senior project for the IB program before graduation. However, being only one person one the team to produce this project and how many ideas I was coming up for it I made the decision to scrap this project for the time being until I can complete it. This project was using the RPG Maker MV engine to make a more of 8bit style adventure game full of different arcing story lines and many side quests to complete.

Shattered Reality is about a child named Ara trying to find himself after a car crash. The boy lives in his headspace with his two friends Rae and Eli going on adventures to protect the people of headspace from courped rulers. After each kingdom they face and bring back to how it was before Ara discovers more and more of what happened to him. Untimatly having to decide in the end whether to accept everything and return back to the real world waking up from this dream or staying in the headspace he created to sheild him from whatever the outside world has in store for him.

The plans for this project in the future are to complete it and put it on stream hopefully getting a huge player base from this endeavor.
