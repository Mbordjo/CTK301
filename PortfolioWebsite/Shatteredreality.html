About

Shattered Reality was the first game idea I tried to put pen to paper and produce. It was supposed to be for my final senior project for the IB program before graduation. However, being only one person on the team to create this project and how many ideas I was coming up for it, I decided to scrap this project for the time being until I could complete it. This project used the RPG Maker MV engine to make a more 8-bit adventure game full of different arcing storylines and many side quests to complete.

Shattered Reality is about a child named Ara who is trying to find himself after a car crash. The boy lives in his headspace with his two friends, Rae and Eli, going on adventures to protect the people of headspace from corrupted rulers. After each kingdom, they face and bring back how it was before Ara discovers more of what happened to him. Ultimately, he has to decide in the end whether to accept everything and return to the real world, waking up from this dream or staying in the headspace he created to shield him from whatever the outside world has in store for him.

The plans for this project are to complete it and put it on stream, hopefully getting a vast player base from this endeavor.
