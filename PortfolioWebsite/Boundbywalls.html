About

Bound by Walls is a 2D singleplayer game I made with my friend Deven I met on discord. This was the frist time I participated in a game jam on itch.io and the frist time I had a partner for a game project to complete it on time. I was the Head Artist for this game while my friend was the Head of Programming because he knew the engine (GDevelop5) better than I did. To make the art for this project I was using asprite on my computer.

Bound by Walls is about a boy named Goose, your role in this game is to make sure that Goose doesn't leave the house out of bordom because the game jam theme was "do not go outside". So you have to keep Goose enteratined by doing activities around the house like sleeping, watching tv, taking a shower, and more.

Our plans for this project in the future are to fix all of the bugs and input new more well put together art and add more gameplay by interating with more objects in the house.
