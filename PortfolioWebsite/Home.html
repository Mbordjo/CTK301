Artist Statement

My art illuminates the often misunderstood nature of mental illness, using a diverse array of mediums including game design, photography, painting, sketching, 3d modeling and other mixed media to educate and bring advocacy to this topic. I aim to destigmatize mental health issues and foster understanding about the topic and show empathy for those who suffer with the mental illness shown in my work.

Each piece I make has layers of meaning, inviting exploration and interpretation to all who see it. My creative process is both spontaneous and deliberate, allowing for unusual sources of inspiration and expression while maintaining a deep value of technique. A testament to the resilience of the human spirit in the face of uncertainly for what is upcoming.

My goal is to not only to raise awareness but also to spark meaningful conversations and inspire action towards greater compassion and support for those affected by mental illness.
