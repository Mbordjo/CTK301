About

This project was from a school assignment I had to do for my introduction to my major course. This assignment was to vandalize something on campus, but it could not have been anything negative and should be able to be taken down if needed. This project was assigned right after a breakup with my partner at the time but conveniently before Halloween. I got the idea while interacting with friends online through Discord, where my username at the time had something to do with ghosts, so I had the idea of putting ghosts around the quad!

So I told some friends I made in that class about my plan, and they helped to pitch out with drawing and printing out about 50-60 ghosts and getting the white string to hang them on the trees around the quad. One of my close friends, Amber, and I were up until 4 a.m. one night just trying to throw the yarn on the trees without a ladder to hang the ghosts up; it was the most fun experience I will ever have.

My plans with this project are to do it again for Halloween in 2024 and to try and do it for Valentine's Day in 2025, too. I wanted to do it in 2024 because some of the ghosts we put up had hearts on them, which would be very cute.
