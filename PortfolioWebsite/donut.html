About

With my first attempt in 3D, I was searching for tutorials on how to learn Blender, and I found a YouTube series by Blender Guru, which was an intro to making 3D models in Blender by starting with creating a donut. This project is the outcome of following the beginning of that tutorial, and it made me confident that I can learn more about Blender and create something unique if I have time.

My plans for this project are to continue following Blender Guru's tutorial and create a scene with multiple donuts on a plate with different sprinkles on them. Also, my plans for 3D models as a whole are to attempt to make a replica of the RB20 (Oracle Red Bull's most recent Formula One car) and make Vtuber models soon.
