About

My Notion School Planner is my first introduction to Notion. I made this Notion an excellent place to organize myself and ensure I am on top of not just school and assignments but daily life. Because I will be living off campus for my sophomore year, it will be essential to be organized, start knowing what I must do every day, and make sure I am on time for everything.

It includes all of my classes for the upcoming semester, when and where they meet, and a place to write notes. It also has an assignment tracker and a meal planner with multiple Pinterest recipes, instructions for cooking, and what ingredients are needed.

The plans for this project are to complete it, add a fitness tracker, add routes to better myself and my body, and clean my apartment.
