About

My Notion School Planner is my frist interduction to Notion. ... I made this Notion to ... Unspoken Words is a visual novel that was for a game jam my major hosted. I was in a group of three with two of my friends from my Web Design course. However it didnt go as I planned it would. It was supposed to be each person has their own role, such as artist, stoory boarder and programmer. But, thats not how it ended up, I did all the story and plot writing and most of the art and all of the programing. the artist person did finally get back to me and gave me one artwork for the mom that was used in the game for the game jam but that was a hour before showing off the game. However it some how worked out. This game was made using the renpy engine.

Includes all of my classes for the upcoming semester, when and where they meet and a place to write notes. It also has an assignment tracker, a meal planner with multiple recipes found on pinterest with instructions of how to cook and what ingredants are needed.

The plans for this project are to complete it, add a fitness tracker and add routes to better myself and my body as well as routes for cleaning my appartment.
