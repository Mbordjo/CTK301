About

The Home Guardian Remake is a project that I was trying to have time for at the beginning of this semester, but I have yet to get the chance to fix it. This project is for my dad's business because when the company came out, he made his own website from scratch, which you can see on the side of one of the working pages. However, because of the advancements in the web, this website doesn't have the charm it used to, and only a few of the pages are working due to corrupted code or loss of the images used. When I have time, this will be one of my top priorities, and I will fix and remake it.

This website is meant to be an information hub of what my father does and his recommended products for customers for security cameras, speakers, and more.

I plan to make the website look more modern and contemporary than the original and have the products be more like a gallery or like Amazon, where they are organized based on what the product is, and if you are looking for something specific, you can search for that product.
